1. On-Device First

AmvoTech builds tools that respect your privacy by design. Our applications, including L2Cache, process data entirely on-device whenever possible. We do not transmit your clipboard history or personal data to our servers.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect telemetry, usage statistics, or crash reports without your explicit, opt-in consent. If you choose to share crash reports via Apple's standard reporting mechanisms, we only use this data to fix bugs and improve our software.

3. Third-Party APIs

Some features may require the use of external APIs (e.g. LLM providers for AI functionality). In these cases, you connect using your own API keys, and your data is transmitted directly to those providers subject to their privacy policies. AmvoTech does not act as a middleman for this data.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@amvo.store.
