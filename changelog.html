v1.0
June 2026
Initial release

L2Cache is here. A clipboard manager that remembers everything you copy, understands what it is, and gets out of your way.

Clipboard
  • Unlimited clipboard history — text, images, URLs, code, files
  • Panel opens in under 100ms via ⌘⇧V
  • Full-text search across all clips
  • Smart albums automatically group clips by type and context
AI
  • AI-generated titles — clips are named by what they mean, not what they are
  • Apple Intelligence on-device categorisation — nothing leaves your Mac
  • Smart Transform — copy smarter: strip markdown, fix grammar, format JSON, convert code
Privacy & Security
  • Sensitive radar — API keys, credit cards, private keys, SSNs, and WiFi credentials automatically detected and locked
  • Unlock sensitive items with Touch ID or your login password
  • App exclusions — stop capturing from any app
  • All data stored locally in SQLite — zero network requests
Extras
  • QR code generation — turn any URL or text into a QR code in one click
  • Screenshot renaming — AI renames screenshots by their content automatically
  • Spotlight integration — find clips without opening the panel
